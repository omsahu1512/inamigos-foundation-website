ABOUT US

Welcome To InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization established on 23 September 2020 with the vision of creating a better and more inclusive society. Founded by Mr. Govind Shukla, the foundation works tirelessly in education, healthcare, environmental conservation, women empowerment, skill development and community welfare.

Through innovative projects, dedicated volunteers and partnerships, the foundation has positively impacted thousands of lives across India.

Explore Our Projects

Our Mission

To uplift underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, environmental awareness, youth empowerment and sustainable development, ensuring equal opportunities for every individual.

Our Vision

To build a compassionate and self-reliant society where every person has access to education, healthcare, dignity and opportunities for a brighter future.

Our Values

Compassion, transparency, innovation, teamwork, equality and sustainable impact remain the core values guiding every initiative undertaken by InAmigos Foundation.

OUR FOUNDER

Leading with Compassion & Purpose

Mr. Govind Shukla, Founder of InAmigos Foundation, established the organization with a vision of empowering communities through education, healthcare, environmental protection and social development. His leadership continues to inspire thousands of volunteers and supporters to work towards building a more inclusive and compassionate society.

Under his guidance, the foundation has expanded its initiatives across multiple states in India, positively impacting the lives of children, women, senior citizens and economically weaker communities.
